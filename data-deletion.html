About Data Deletion

As a Phoenician employee, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data from our HR system. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, contact details, employee ID)
  • Biometric data (facial recognition templates)
  • Location tracking data
  • Attendance records (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • App usage data and logs

Important Notice

Some employment records may be retained as required by UAE Labor Law for up to 7 years. We will delete all data that is not legally required to be retained. Your request will be processed within 30 business days.
