🔒 Facial Recognition and Biometric Data Collection

Important Notice: Our Application uses advanced facial recognition technology for employee authentication and attendance verification.

What We Collect:

  • Facial Images: High-resolution photographs of your face taken during registration
  • Biometric Templates: Mathematical representations of your facial features converted into encrypted data
  • Facial Landmarks: Unique facial geometry points (distance between eyes, nose shape, jawline, etc.)
  • Recognition Patterns: Digital signatures created from your facial characteristics
  • Authentication Attempts: Records of successful and failed face recognition attempts

How Facial Data is Processed:

  • Initial Registration: During first-time setup, the app captures multiple facial images from different angles
  • Template Creation: Your facial images are converted into encrypted biometric templates that cannot be reverse-engineered into photos
  • Secure Storage: Biometric templates are stored on secure company servers with enterprise-grade encryption
  • Real-time Matching: During attendance marking, your live facial scan is compared against your stored template

Security Measures for Facial Data:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All biometric data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • No Image Storage: Original facial photographs are deleted after template creation
  • Access Control: Biometric data is accessible only to authorized system administrators
  • Immediate Deletion: Upon employment termination, all biometric data is permanently deleted within 30 days

Your Rights Regarding Facial Data:

  • Consent Withdrawal: You may withdraw consent for facial recognition at any time with 30-day notice
  • Alternative Authentication: Alternative login methods available if you opt-out of facial recognition
  • Data Access: You can request information about your stored biometric data
  • Breach Notification: You will be immediately notified of any biometric data security incidents
